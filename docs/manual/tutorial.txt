====================================================================
1) TONG QUAN HE THONG
====================================================================

He thong managerCMN gom cac module chinh:
- Dashboard
- Nhan su (Nhan vien, Hop dong)
- Cham cong
- Don tu (tao don, theo doi, sua/huy)
- Duyet don
- Phong hop
- Ticket noi bo
- Tai san
- Thong bao
- Ho so ca nhan
- Danh muc he thong (Admin)
- Nhat ky he thong (Master Admin)

[ANH 01 - Trang tong quan sau khi dang nhap]

--------------------------------------------------------------------
1.1) Cac vai tro su dung
--------------------------------------------------------------------
- User: su dung cac chuc nang co ban (xem dashboard, cham cong, don tu, ticket, thong bao, ho so, tai san cua toi).
- Manager: them quyen quan ly theo phan cong (xem/duyet rong hon, xem danh sach nhan vien theo quyen).
- Admin: quan tri nhan su, hop dong, tai san, danh muc, phan quyen.
- Master Admin: Admin dac biet (ma nhan vien A00000), co them quyen xem Nhat ky he thong va cap Admin tiep theo.

[ANH 02 - Minh hoa menu theo quyen User]
[ANH 03 - Minh hoa menu theo quyen Admin]
